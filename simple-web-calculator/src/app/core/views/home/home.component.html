Instructions:

You can click on the buttons or use your keyboard (focus must be on the calculator). While using the keyboard only the keys below are allowed:

Numbers: 0 to 9; Operators: + (sum), - (subtraction), * (multiplication) and / (division); Commands: 'c' or 'C' (clear) and '='' or 'Enter' (equals);
